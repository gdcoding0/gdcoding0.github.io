The ins and outs of bestday

What is bestday?

Great question! And you've probably asked this question before, maybe many times before.

If you've ever missed a deadline on a project, spent too long sending an email that didn't really matter, enmeshed in unenlightening conversation, put off starting your couch to 5k in favour an easier but unfufilling shortcut. Maybe you forgot to pay a bill on time or prioritized stuff that didn't need as much time as you gave to it. If the above sounds like you (if only a little bit) - you've asked this question and you've experienced the consequences. That looming feeling when we could have got more out of the day and more importantly, more out of ourselves. We did things that simply were not important enough and not really aligned with our best interests.

The day slips away and we get less done.

bestday is an app with a very simple mission statement: to give you the very best tools, practices and skills to take back your day. To empower you and your goals. And to put you back at the center of your universe.

This is done by bringing together all the very best in effective personal management and effectiveness while making the process of comprehension, implementation and integration as straightforward as possible. That is our promise to you.

The rest is down to you!

Who is bestday for?

Everyone! Yes, everyone!

Squeezing as much potential out of yourself and your day is something we all can do.

Everyone can benefit from boosting their personal effectiveness and productivity.

Whether you are looking to gain the most of your day for personal or professional reasons. Whether your goals are big or small. Whatever it is you are doing, the day starts and ends with you.

And so we believe you should get the most out of it and finish feeling like you accomplished more!

How does it work?

Simple! When you tell bestday what is most important you, bestday will create a plan around this so you can maximize your day!

That's it!

How does it work?

bestday works by what is most important to you.

To begin, you choose what really matters to you and this is then used to create a plan which aims to build your best day (that's where the name comes from!).
