Y Club Wrestling Website Draft

This is just a draft for what the website could look like. It is not functional.

Imagery All the images can be changed, as well as the logo.

Dynamic Content / WordPress The two boxes below the team photo at the top of the page represent content that can be dynamically changed using WordPress. Right now they are static and just represent dummy posts. They exist as placeholders for content related to the goings-on with the club and will be changeable very easily by anyone who has a login for the backend and can create new posts.

Current State Right now the draft only works on desktops and larger devices and will 'break' on smaller ones like smartphones and tablets. Mobile version on it's way.
